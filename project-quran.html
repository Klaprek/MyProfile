Quran App

Overview

A beautiful and feature-rich Quran application built with Flutter, providing an immersive experience for reading and listening to the Holy Quran.

Features

Surah View: The application displays a complete list of all surahs in the Quran. Each surah has a detailed view that includes the Arabic text, transliteration, and translation. Users can easily navigate to specific verses within a surah.

Juz View: The app provides a full list of all 30 juz of the Quran. Each juz is broken down in detail by surah and verse for better understanding and navigation.

Bookmark & Last Read: Users can save their favorite verses as bookmarks for quick access later. The app also keeps track of the last-read position, allowing users to continue their recitation seamlessly. Bookmarks and last-read entries can be deleted via long-press actions.

Customizable Settings: Users can adjust the font size for Arabic text, transliteration, and translation. They can also toggle the visibility of transliteration and translation. The app allows the selection of a preferred translation language and audio reciter. It supports both dark and light display modes.

Verse Audio: Each verse can be played with audio features that include play, pause, resume, and stop functions. Multiple reciters are available to enhance the listening experience.
